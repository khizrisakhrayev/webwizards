Website Project!

The website project was about creating a dynamic website using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Before collaborating to create our website, each team member diligently completed individual parts to master these essential languages. During the individual part, we learned from structuring web pages with HTML tags to styling them with CSS and adding interactivity with JavaScript.

With enthusiasm and determination, our team of WebWizards was formed. Under the leadership of Khizri Sakhrayev, we recognized the significance of effective communication and teamwork. Github became our primary platform to make daily commitments to our project. Despite initial challenges, we quickly got used to working on the GitHub Platform.
Building our first website was both interesting and challenging. We subsequently created each page and gave them an appealing style. In some cases, we faced difficulties in converting our ideas into code. Therefore, we conducted more research and learned new techniques to solve the issues.
At the end of the project, we gained broad knowledge about building websites. Moving forward, we carry with us this experience to tackle new challenges and continue to take part in new projects. As WebWizards, we look forward to applying our skills to create more dynamic and impactful websites in the future.
